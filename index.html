Cross-Linguistic Semantic Alignment for Universal Philosophical Concepts

What are the truly universal philosophical concepts with which we should philosophize?

Cross-Linguistic Semantic Alignment for Universal Philosophical Concepts is a research project that aims to explore philosophical concepts that are not English-centric and are truly universal by examining and analyzing philosophical concepts in languages around the world, combining empirical research in experimental philosophy with analysis using machine learning and large language models (LLMs), and actually constructing and submitting concepts that are close to the "center."
